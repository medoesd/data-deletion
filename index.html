Facebook Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data associated with our app, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings.
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites".
  3. Find our app in the list and click "Remove".
  4. Once removed, all data associated with your account will be deleted from our servers.
